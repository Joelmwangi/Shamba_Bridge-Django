Alot of people ask a lot of question about us.

What is Shamba Bridge?

Shamba Bridge is an online platform designed to connect farmers with agricultural experts and resources, providing guidance on best farming practices and facilitating direct market access.

How can I join Shamba Bridge??

You can sign up for free by visiting our website and creating an account. Choose the package that suits your needs, and start accessing valuable resources and community support.

What features are included in the free Basic Package?

The Basic Package includes access to educational resources, participation in community forums, connections to agricultural experts, and regular newsletters with farming tips.

What additional benefits does the Standard Package offer?

The Standard Package includes all features of the Basic Package, plus online bookkeeping tools to help manage your farm finances and professional site visits from our specialists for personalized support.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, there are no hidden fees. The pricing for each package is transparent, and you will know exactly what you are paying for each month.

Is my data secure on the Shamba Bridge platform?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your personal and financial information is protected with advanced security measures, ensuring confidentiality and safety.
